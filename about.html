01 — Biography

Hi, I’m Shakeel Ahmad.

I’m a Licensed Real Estate Salesperson with Platinum Properties, based at 2270 Latta Road in Greece, NY. I help buyers and sellers move forward with clarity and confidence across Monroe County, the Finger Lakes, and the wider Upstate New York region — and I support clients relocating to and from New York City.

A meaningful part of my practice is probate and time-sensitive estate sales. These transactions often arrive during difficult moments, and they deserve patience, discretion, and careful coordination with attorneys and family members. I treat every client’s situation as if it were my own.

Whatever your move, my promise is simple: clear communication, honest counsel, and a calm, well-organized process from our first conversation through closing.
